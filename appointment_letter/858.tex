the period he/she has not taken any Assignment.

\begin{enumerate}[label=\textbf{(\alph*)}, itemsep=3pt, topsep=2pt]

        responsible person in the Customer's organization as to the services to be performed during
        an Assignment.
\end{enumerate}

\end{enumerate}
